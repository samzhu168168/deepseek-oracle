## DESIGN PRINCIPLES (Apply to all 4 products)

**Visual language:**
- Dark, rich backgrounds: deep navy (#0d0f1a), obsidian black, midnight purple
- Accent colors: warm gold, ember orange, cool silver, jade green — by element
- Typography overlay: Modern serif (Cormorant Garamond style) in gold/white
- Texture: subtle grain, ancient paper, ink wash, constellation maps
- Mood: intimate oracle aesthetic — not occult, not corporate

**What to avoid:**
- Chinese flags, dragon imagery, explicitly "Asian" iconography (repositioned as universal wisdom)
- Clipart-style zodiac symbols
- Bright, playful colors
- Stock photo people (faces)

**What to include:**
- Abstract elemental textures (fire, water, wood grain, earth stone, metal sheen)
- Geometric sacred geometry overlaid subtly
- Minimal text overlay: product name + "elemental.bond"

## PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

**Step 1:** Generate all A variants in Midjourney first (use /imagine commands above)
**Step 2:** If Midjourney quality is insufficient, use DALL-E 3 C variants
**Step 3:** Download 4 best images
**Step 4:** Open in Canva (1280×720 template)
**Step 5:** Add text overlay using specifications above
**Step 6:** Export as PNG (not JPEG) for Gumroad upload
**Step 7:** Upload to each Gumroad product page as cover image

**Canva Template Setup:**
- Background: Upload generated image as full bleed
- Text layer 1: Product title — Cormorant Garamond Bold, 72px, #d4a853 (gold)
- Text layer 2: Subtitle — Cormorant Garamond Regular Italic, 32px, #f0e8d8
- Text layer 3: elemental.bond — Inter Regular, 20px, #a0a8b8 (silver-gray)
- Add subtle dark gradient overlay at bottom (30% opacity) to ensure text readability
