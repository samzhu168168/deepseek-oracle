# Video Script Generator

## Overview

Generate TikTok/Reels/Shorts scripts (30-60s) that make ancient Eastern divination feel modern, personal, and irresistible to US audiences.

## Brand Voice

- Warm, wise, but never preachy
- Science-adjacent ("2000 years of pattern recognition")
- Curiosity-driven, not fear-driven
- Modern metaphors over ancient jargon
- Inclusive and universal

## Writing Rules

1. Hook must create a gap between what viewer knows and what they want to know
2. Never say "ancient Chinese secret" — say "2000-year-old pattern system"
3. Every script must answer: "Why should I care right now?"
4. CTA must feel like an invitation, not an ad
5. Subtitle text must be readable at 2x speed on mute
6. Avoid all Chinese terms unless immediately explained in English
7. Numbers and statistics make claims feel credible
8. End with a question that lingers
