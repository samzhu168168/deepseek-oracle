### For The Oracle: 5 Battle-Tested Hooks

#### Hook 1: The Identity Attack
> **"If you were born between 1996-2001 — stop scrolling."**

- *Why it works:* Specificity = credibility. Date range triggers "is that me?" reflex.
- *Visual:* Black screen → single date range appears → ◈ symbol flashes

#### Hook 2: The Pain Mirror
> **"Your last 3 relationships ended the same way. That's not bad luck."**

- *Why it works:* Calls out a universal but unspoken experience. Viewers feel exposed.
- *Visual:* Fast cuts of couple arguing → freeze on hand pulling away

#### Hook 3: The Counter-Narrative
> **"Therapists tell you to 'communicate more.' They're wrong."**

- *Why it works:* Attacks trusted authority. Creates instant controversy/curiosity.
- *Visual:* Therapy couch in crosshairs → cut to The Oracle symbol

#### Hook 4: The Future Threat
> **"May 2026. Your relationship hits a wall. Do you know which week?"**

- *Why it works:* Specific timeframe + negative consequence = must-watch
- *Visual:* Calendar flipping rapidly → stops on May → red circle around a date

#### Hook 5: The Secret Knowledge
> **"There's a compatibility code in your birth chart. 99% of people never see it."**

- *Why it works:* Insider knowledge = status. "I want to be the 1%."
- *Visual:* Ancient symbols → zoom into one glowing character

## Part 4: Production Tools & Workflow

### The Stack (All Free / Open Source)

| Tool | Type | Use |
|------|------|-----|
| **DaVinci Resolve** | Free (not OSS) | Professional video editor — best for YouTube long-form |
| **CapCut Desktop** | Free | Best for TikTok/Reels/Shorts — auto-captions, templates |
| **Kdenlive** | Open Source | If you insist on FOSS — decent editor, text overlays, keyframes |
| **OBS Studio** | Open Source | Screen/face recording |
| **Pexels / Pixabay** | Free stock | Download ambient background videos (ink, smoke, fire, water) |
| **ElevenLabs** | Free tier (10K chars) | AI voiceover — most natural English voices |
| **Audacity** | Open Source | Audio recording/editing |
| **Canva** | Free tier | Quote cards, Pinterest pins, thumbnails |

### Recommended Workflow (10 minutes per video)

```
STEP 1: Record AI VO (2 min)
  → ElevenLabs, paste the script
  → Voice: "Adam" or "Antoni" (deep, authoritative, Oracle-like)
  → Export as .mp3

STEP 2: Gather Background Footage (3 min)
  → Pexels.com → search "ink water", "smoke", "candle", "fire closeup"
  → Download 3-5 clips, 10-15 seconds each

STEP 3: Assemble in CapCut (4 min)
  → Import background clips
  → Import audio VO
  → Add text overlays (CapCut auto-captions)
  → Apply dark cinematic filter
  → Adjust timing — words appear on beat

STEP 4: Export & Post (1 min)
  → 1080×1920 for Reels/TikTok
  → 1920×1080 for YouTube Shorts
```

### EleventLabs Settings for The Oracle Voice

- **Voice:** "Adam" (deep, resonant, slightly aged)
- **Stability:** 65% (less = more expressive)
- **Clarity:** 85%
- **Style Exaggeration:** 30% (don't over-dramatize)
- **Speed:** 0.95x (slightly slower — ancient wisdom doesn't rush)
