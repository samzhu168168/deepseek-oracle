# Visual Direction — The Oracle

## Brand Aesthetic

**NOT:** Pastel crystals, tarot cards, galaxy backgrounds, AI-generated spiritual art

**YES:** Dark, warm, grounded. Think:
- Ink on aged paper
- Deep charcoal + gold + cream
- The ◈ symbol as your brand mark
- Minimal, high-contrast, editorial

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Oracle Black | `#1a1a1a` | Backgrounds, text |
| Oracle Gold | `#c9a96e` | Accents, CTA buttons |
| Oracle Cream | `#f5f0e8` | Card backgrounds |
| Oracle Red | `#8b1a1a` | Emphasis (use sparingly) |

## Font Pairing

- **Headlines:** Cormorant Garamond (serif, elegant, ancient feel)
- **Body:** Inter or DM Sans (clean, modern, readable)

## Quote Card Template (Canva instructions)

1. **Background:** Solid `#1a1a1a` or subtle paper texture
2. **Top:** Small ◈ symbol in gold `#c9a96e`
3. **Middle:** Quote in Cormorant Garamond, 36pt, cream `#f5f0e8`, centered
4. **Bottom:** "— The Oracle" in Inter, 14pt, gold
5. **Bottom-right:** `elemental.bond` in Inter, 11pt, cream 60% opacity

## Reels/TikTok Visual Style

- **No talking head** needed (use text-on-screen + mystical ambient background video)
- Background sources: ink in water, smoke, candle flame, rain on window
- Font overlays: white text on dark background, 2-3 words at a time
- Sound: ambient/dark cinematic (not trending pop songs)
- Speed: fast cuts, 1-2 seconds per phrase

## Profile Picture / Brand Mark

The ◈ symbol on a dark background. Simple. Recognizable. Mysterious.
